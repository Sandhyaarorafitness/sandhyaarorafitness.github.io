FAQs

Common Questions

Do you offer online fitness training?

Yes! All services including 1-on-1 coaching, group classes, and structured programs are available fully online via video sessions and app-based tracking — from anywhere in India or abroad.

What's the best program for weight loss?

The 12-Week Total Transformation Program combines customised workouts with nutrition coaching and accountability check-ins for maximum sustainable fat loss and body recomposition.

Can complete beginners join?

Absolutely. Sandhya specialises in meeting clients at every fitness level. Complete beginners are warmly welcome — sessions and programs are always calibrated to your current fitness.

Do I need gym equipment for online training?

No gym required! Programs can be designed for home workouts with minimal equipment, or fully equipped gym sessions. You tell us what you have, and the plan adapts to you.

How soon will I see results?

Most clients notice visible changes in energy, strength, and body composition within the first 3–4 weeks. Significant transformation results are typically visible by 8–12 weeks with consistency.

Is nutrition coaching included in programs?

Yes — all structured programs include a personalised nutrition framework. Detailed standalone nutrition coaching is also available as a separate service for those focused primarily on diet.
